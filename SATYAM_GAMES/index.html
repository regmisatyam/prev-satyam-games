Phonebook

"Phonebook" is a comprehensive mobile application that serves as a vital resource for users in times of emergency. It includes a well compiled list of essential contact details, such as emergency phone numbers, hotlines, and comprehensive information for immediate assistance from services like the police, ambulance, fire brigade, and traffic authorities. With Phonebook, users have quick and easy access to these critical contact details, ensuring their safety and peace of mind during unexpected situations.

Hangman: Word Guess

"Hangman: Word Guess" is an engaging word puzzle game that challenges players to guess hidden words one letter at a time. Players are presented with a series of dashes representing the letters of a secret word, along with a category hint to provide a clue. They must guess letters, one by one, in an attempt to uncover the word correctly. However, there's a catch – for each incorrect guess, a part of a hanging figure, typically a "hangman," is drawn, bringing players closer to losing the game. It's a fun and educational game that tests vocabulary and logic while providing an enjoyable and suspenseful gaming experience.

Guess By Image

"Guess By Image" is the ultimate logo guessing game that puts your brand knowledge to the test! Dive into a world of over 1000 iconic logos from various industries. Challenge yourself with multiple difficulty levels, use hints when needed, and immerse yourself in visually stunning graphics. Learn the history and significance of each logo as you play. Compete with friends, climb the leaderboard, and showcase your logo recognition prowess. Whether you're a logo enthusiast, trivia lover, or just looking for fun with family and friends, "Guess By Image" offers an engaging and educational gaming experience. Download now and start guessing!
